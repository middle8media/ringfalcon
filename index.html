The Ring Falcon

2010

The one that started it all. This short film was written, filmed & edited within 7 hours, as a creative challenge on a bright and sunny Saturday morning. When it premiered that same night, it was an instant hit and the kids immediately started rallying around The Ring Falcon II.

The Ring Falcon II

2011

The second installment of this epic trilogy introduces a few new characters on screen, as well as the Narrator. Filmed in 3 hours and edited over a week, it premiered on a cool evening and became an instant classic. The Ring Falcon III was ineveitable.

The Ring Falcon III

2012

As The Ring Falcon became more popular around the community, this final installment began to take on a life of it's own. Filmed in a handful of weekends throughout the year, this short film included 9 families with a total of 21 kids. Whew, this adventure was literally EPICALLY AWESOME!
